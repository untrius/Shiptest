Simulated overmap ship
/obj/structure/overmap/ship/simulated

A ship that corresponds to an actual, physical shuttle.

Can be docked to any other overmap object with a corresponding docking port and/or zlevel. SUPPOSED to be linked to the corresponding shuttle's mobile docking port, but you never know do you
